2. Target Audience

Target audience

The target audience is all the people who visit the selected three regions (user can preset the regions up to three), or the people who live in the region. The information provided is helpful for not only outdoor-activity lovers, but also for people living there. For example, when people do washing clothes, wash cars, etc., or when people are suffering weather-dependent diseases such as headaches, hay-fevers, etc.


Persona 1

  • Ms Apple
    • She is a constructor who visited the town for the newly planned building. She needs to see the scheduled construction place to negotiate details with her cliant.
  • Age: 40

Persona 2

  • Mr Brwon
  • A resident of the town. He washes clothes daily and loves to dry them in the open air in the sunshine. He also walks to a nearby park with his dog every morning and evening.
  • Age: 60


Scenarios

  • 1. A boy (10 years old) in the town is planning to go fishing with his father this weekend. He and his father want to know the weather to select the fishing gear and their clothes. Also, general lake information and traffic information provided at this website will be helpful for them.

  • 2. The event organizers who will have an outdoor market need time-dependent weather information to prepare the materials to distribute. He will prepare foods and drinks depending on the temperature. So he checks the site.

  • 3. A boy scout leader needs to check the weather forecast to decide about their troop hiking plan which is planned for this Saturday. Not only general weather information, but pinpointed weather forecast of the mountain area provided by our website is useful for them.

  • 4. This lady living in the town is considering whether she should dry the washed sheets outside the house or indoors. Today's weather forecast with humidity information is helpful for her.

  • 5. Elaine suffers from headaches when atmospheric pressure is becoming low. From the forecast, she knew she was getting a headache today, so she took a pill beforehand to attend an important meeting.
