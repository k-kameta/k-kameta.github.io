2. Target Audience

Target audience

The target audience is all the people who visit the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea. The site provide information on this region to enjoy outdoor lives. Lovers of open air atomospher are all welcome who surely like services the company provides - rental scooters and Jeeps.


Persona 1

  • Ms Apple
    • She is a office worker who is currently planning to visit islands during this summer in Mexico. She loves outside activityes and would like to move around by herself. She will come and use the services with her friend of the same age and wants to use the site for her planning and booking.
  • Age: 32


Persona 2

  • Mr Brwon
  • He is hard worker in New York. He want to have a relaxed time in this autumn season in Mexico. Therefore, the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexico seems the best place for his solo-trip. He rests in the open air and can recover his energy. By using a Jeep, he can easily move anywhere in the island by himself.
  • Age: 45


Scenarios

  • 1. A family in Utah is planning to go a week-longadventure near sea side. They love outside activities and want to use a Jeep to move around the island. They want to research the places to go and want to get a week-long weather forcast.

  • 2. A couple form Arizona want to have a relaxed trip in the region of Cozumel. Scooters are best for them to move around the town. They also want to get the information for the best places to visit.

  • 3. A boy scout leader needs to check the weather forecast to decide about their troop hiking plan which is planned in this weekend. Not only general weather information, but pinpointed weather forecast of the off shore area provided by the website is useful for them to plan marine activities.
